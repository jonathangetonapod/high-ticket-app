# Email Copy Standards

## Spintax Requirements

- **Spintax is REQUIRED** in all email bodies
- Use format: `{option1|option2|option3}`
- Minimum 3 spintax variations per email
- Spintax should vary greetings, transitions, and CTAs

## Spam Trigger Words to Avoid

Never use these words/phrases:

- "Free" / "100% free"
- "Act now" / "Limited time"
- "Click here" / "Click below"
- "Guarantee" / "Guaranteed"
- "No obligation"
- "Winner" / "Congratulations"
- ALL CAPS sentences
- Excessive exclamation marks!!!

## Personalization Requirements

Every email MUST include:

1. `{{first_name}}` or equivalent
2. Company reference (`{{company}}` or natural mention)
3. Relevant pain point or context

## Link Guidelines

- Maximum 2 links per email
- No link in first email (text-only recommended)
- Calendar links should use proper tracking domains
- Avoid URL shorteners (bit.ly, etc.)

## Subject Line Rules

- Keep under 50 characters
- No spam trigger words
- Personalization encouraged
- No ALL CAPS
- No excessive punctuation
