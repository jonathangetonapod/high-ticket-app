# Loom Video Requirements

## Purpose

The Loom video is your chance to explain your work and demonstrate strategic thinking. It's reviewed by the delivery team.

## Required Duration

- **Minimum:** 3 minutes
- **Maximum:** 7 minutes
- Sweet spot: 4-5 minutes

## What to Cover

### 1. Client Context (30-60 seconds)
- Who is the client?
- What do they sell?
- Brief recap of strategy call highlights

### 2. ICP Explanation (60 seconds)
- Who are we targeting?
- Why this ICP?
- How does it connect to the strategy call?

### 3. Lead List Overview (60 seconds)
- How many leads?
- Where did they come from?
- Any ICP adjustments made and why?

### 4. Email Sequence Walkthrough (60-90 seconds)
- Walk through each email
- Explain the logic/flow
- Highlight personalization strategy

### 5. Expected Results (30 seconds)
- What response rate do you expect?
- Any concerns or flags?

## Quality Standards

- Clear audio (use a decent mic)
- Show your screen while explaining
- No background distractions
- Professional tone

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Reading the email copy word-for-word
- Not explaining WHY you made certain choices
- Skipping the ICP justification
- Mumbling or rushing through
- Videos under 3 minutes

## Blockers

Submission blocked if:

1. No Loom URL provided
2. Video is under 2 minutes
3. Video is private/inaccessible
